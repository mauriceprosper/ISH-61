About Us

Championing Human Rights for a
Just and Inclusive Society in Africa

ISH-61 Human Rights & Social Justice Initiative is a Nigerian organization committed to protecting human rights for vulnerable groups, including children, women, and persons with disabilities. Our mission is to create an egalitarian society through legal representation, advocacy, research, and training. We focus on various rights, including socio-economic and digital rights, striving for equal access to justice for all.
